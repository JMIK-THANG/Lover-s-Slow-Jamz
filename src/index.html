      Romantic Charleston: A Valentine’s Day Escape by the Sea, History, and Nature

      Folly Beach offers a romantic seaside escape with stunning sunsets and peaceful ocean waves, perfect for a Valentine’s Day stroll. Charleston’s Waterfront Park enchants couples with its iconic Pineapple Fountain, charming swings, and breathtaking harbor views. The majestic Angel Oak Tree adds a fairy-tale touch, creating a magical and unforgettable setting for love and connection.
